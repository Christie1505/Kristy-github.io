Fresh Grapefruit Cake

When citrus is at its peak flavor in Florida, I pull out this grapefruit cake recipe. The tangy fruit adds a refreshing flavor to each bite.

Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 25 mins Additional Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 55 mins Servings: 12 Yield: 2 9-inch cake layers

Ingredients

3 cups sifted cake flour

2 ½ teaspoons baking powder

½ teaspoon salt

1 ¾ cups white sugar

⅔ cup butter, softened

2 large eggs

1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract

1 teaspoon grated grapefruit zest

¾ cup milk

½ cup fresh grapefruit juice

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two (9-inch) pans.

Step 2

Sift together flour, baking powder and salt in a medium bowl. Set aside.

Step 3

Cream together sugar and butter in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in vanilla and grapefruit zest. Add flour mixture alternately with milk and grapefruit juice, beating briefly after each addition. Pour batter into the prepared pans.

step 4

Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean, 25 to 30 minutes. Let cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.

Pig Pickin' Cake

This pig pickin' cake is a wonderful, easy-to-make dessert. Three layers get topped with frozen whipped topping and pineapple. I got the recipe from a lady in the Tennessee mountains.

Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 25 mins Total Time: 45 mins

Servings: 10 Yield: 1 (8-inch) layer cake

Ingredients

Cake:

1 (15.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix

1 (11 ounce) can mandarin oranges with juice

4 large eggs

¼ cup vegetable oil

Frosting:

1 (16 ounce) package frozen whipped topping, thawed

1 (15 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained

1 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step 2

Prepare the cake: Mix together cake mix, mandarin oranges with juice, eggs, and oil. Pour batter into three greased and floured 8-inch round pans. Layers will be thin.

Step 3

Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of cake comes out clean, 25 to 30 minutes. Cool layers on wire racks.

Step 4

Make the frosting: Mix together whipped topping, crushed pineapple, and instant pudding mix.

Step 5

Spread frosting onto the cooled cake layers. Stack layers; frost top and sides. Refrigerate until ready to eat.

Step 6

Slice and enjoy!
